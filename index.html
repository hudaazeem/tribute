A Good Girl's Guide to Murder

By Holly Jackson

This is a tribute website to the A Good Girl's Guide to Murder series. This series has 4 books, a prequel novella and three main novels. The protagonist is Pippa (Pip) Fitz-Amobi, a high school senior investigating a cold case. These books have won multiple awards, and the first book has a six-part television adaptation made by the BBC. Explore the website to find more info about the series!
